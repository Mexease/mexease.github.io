About Loop's Notebook

What is Loop's Notebook?

This project was inspired by our mastermind group sessions in ESP 351. We realized many of us faced a common challenge: while we had access to a wide range of creativity tools, we often struggled to remember how to use them or locate relevant examples in our notes.

With my physical notebook wearing out and graduation approaching, I saw the need for a lasting solution. That’s why I created this digital notebook: a resource designed for current students, future classes, and graduates who want to continue using these tools in both their careers and personal lives.

A Message from Dr. Friedman & Bethany

This isn’t a cheat sheet. If you’re looking for shortcuts or trying to get by with minimal effort, you’re in the wrong place. But if you’re committed to understanding and mastering these tools, you’ve found exactly what you need.

Did you take ESP 252 or ESP 351, lose your notes, or struggle to fully grasp the concepts? We’ve been there too—and we understand how mastering these tools can set you apart in ways you might not expect.

This isn’t about memorizing answers; it’s about learning to think in new and creative ways. By embracing the "second right answer," you’ll challenge conventional wisdom, expand your creative boundaries, and unlock career opportunities that might have once seemed out of reach.

If you’re ready to put in the work, these tools can help you rise above the crowd—not just among your peers, but across the entire industry. Be bold. Innovate. Succeed on your own terms.
